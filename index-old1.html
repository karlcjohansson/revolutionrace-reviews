Monthly Review Volume — 2021 to Mar 2026

Collection dropped steadily through 2025 (10K → 1K/month), then exploded in Feb/Mar 2026 (7.8K → 38.3K).

⚠ Data quality flag — Feb 19 → Mar 31, 2026: Daily review counts jumped from ~50/day to 800-2,800/day for ~5 weeks, then returned to baseline. Top 20 single-day spikes in 8 years of history all fall within this window. Consistent with a queue catch-up / platform migration — the publishedAt timestamp likely reflects when reviews entered the new system, not when customers wrote them. Treat Feb-Mar 2026 totals as suspect. The bars below are rendered in a muted hatched pattern.

Rating Distribution by Year — the standout finding

5★ share climbed from 67.2% (2020) to 78.2% (2025) → 80.2% (2026 YTD). 1★ ratings halved over the same period.

✓ Quality signal: Average rating has trended up every year since 2020 (4.52 → 4.67). 2026 YTD is running at 4.72 — the strongest in company history.

Rating Trend by Market — 2023 vs 2025

Every significant market improved or held steady between 2023 and 2025. Norwegian (+0.13) and French (+0.07) gained the most; Finnish remains the lowest-rated market at 4.46 ★.

🌍 Uniform improvement: Unusual to see every market move in the same direction — suggests a company-wide product or service improvement rather than a localized fix.

Review Concentration (Pareto) — broader catalog

Top 20 products drive 38.9% of reviews, top 100 drive 68.9%. Less concentrated than typical — the catalog has deeper mid-tail penetration.

Day-of-Week Pattern

Weekend peak: Sunday is #1, Fri–Sat close behind. Unusual for e-commerce reviews (most sites peak Thu–Fri).

Verified Flag Coverage

Verified-purchase tracking started in 2020 and became 100% from 2021 onward. Pre-2020 reviews are legacy/unverified.

Geographic Mix (by review language)
